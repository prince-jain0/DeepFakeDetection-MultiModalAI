About the Project

In an era where digital manipulation is becoming increasingly sophisticated, deepfake videos pose a serious threat to information integrity, privacy, and security. Our project is designed to detect deepfake videos by analyzing both audio and visual components to distinguish between authentic and manipulated content. How it Works? Visual Analysis: Our model examines facial expressions, inconsistencies in lighting, and frame distortions to identify artificially generated elements. Audio Analysis: The system detects anomalies in speech patterns, mismatched lip synchronization, and unnatural sound modulations. Combined Deepfake Detection: By aligning both audio and video using techniques like Dynamic Time Warping (DTW) and deep learning models, our system provides an accurate deepfake detection result. User-Friendly Interface: The platform allows users to simply upload a video, and the AI-powered system processes it to determine whether it is real or fake. This technology is crucial in combating misinformation, ensuring media authenticity, and preventing malicious usage of deepfake videos in scams, identity theft, and political propaganda.
